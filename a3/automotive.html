2008 schwinn ecotour

work in progress

why tho

I bought this bike off fb marketplace after my previous bike (~2017 lime green diadora mountain bike from my dad) got stolen, decided to try an ebike as my car needed a safety inspection. it had the batteries rebuilt in aug 2022, and had been sitting in storage for a while before that, as it had the 15 YEAR OLD FACTORY TIRES ON IT.

Stats

  • Based off a 08 Currie tech rmb ebike system: manual mirror (not exact model, but close enough)
  • has a sticker on bottom with a date of 11/24/2008 - almost 15 years old
  • MOTOR: 24v w/ integrated gearbox, output 480rpm 9 tooth sprocket, to 20in. rear wheel w/ 20tooth sprocket, top speed of ~21kmh
  • Battery 2x 24v packs, 2x 12v 9ah sla golfcart batteries in each
  • Controller: aftermarket from prev. owner, removes pedal assist(PAS), has unused connector for onboard battery charging
  • Brakes: Generic cable driven rim-grip style v-brakes
  • Frame: roadbike/cruiser frame, springs in front fork, no rear suspension, has heavy duty mounts for rear rack, and motor mounts on left rear fork
  • Pedal: 3 gears on pedals (todo: count teeth on sprockets and calc ratio)
